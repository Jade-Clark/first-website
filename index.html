How to make souffles in our way.

1,Unsalted Butter-Prefer grass-fed butter.
2,All-Purpose Flour-Whole wheat flour or spelt flour should work fine.
3,Large Eggs-I use local free-range eggs.
4,Milk of Choice-Preferably whole milk for a creamy egg texture.
5,Grated Cheese-I like a combination of Emmental cheese and Parmesan cheese.
6,Kosher Salt-I barely salt soufflés, because Parmesan is very salty already.
7,Nutmeg-The French touch that all mums always add to a Soufflé recipe.
